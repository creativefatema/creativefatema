About the studio

Eight years of designing things people remember.

I'm a graphic designer with 8+ years of hands-on experience helping brands, startups and online sellers look their best. My work spans logo and brand identity, social media kits, packaging mockups, vector illustration and photo retouching — always polished to a premium, client-ready finish.

Alongside client projects on Fiverr, Upwork and local commissions, I maintain a growing portfolio of commercial-ready graphics on Adobe Stock, Shutterstock and iStock — so every file I deliver is built to professional, marketplace-grade standards.

  • Brand identity & logo design
  • Social media design kits
  • Vector & stock illustration
  • Packaging & print design
  • Photo editing & retouching
  • Microstock-ready graphics
